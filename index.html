What is Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS)?

Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) is an FDA-approved, non-invasive therapy that uses gentle magnetic pulses to stimulate underactive areas of the brain involved in mood regulation. For many people who haven't found relief with traditional medications or therapy alone, TMS can open the door to meaningful recovery.

  • Non-Invasive Treatment: No surgery, no anesthesia required. TMS is completely external and safe.
  • Medication-Free: No systemic side effects. TMS works directly on the brain without affecting the rest of your body.
  • Comfortable Sessions: Quick 20-minute sessions that you can fit into your daily routine.
  • No Downtime: Resume normal activities immediately after each treatment session.

What to Expect During Treatment

Your first step is a consultation with our team to confirm if TMS is right for you. After approval, here's what the treatment process looks like:

1

Mapping Session

We locate the precise areas of the brain to target for your specific condition.

2

Treatment Sessions

You'll sit in a comfortable chair while a magnetic coil delivers gentle pulses. Each session lasts about 20 minutes.

3

Treatment Schedule

Typically 5 sessions per week for 6–8 weeks, depending on your individual treatment plan.

4

Daily Life

No downtime — you can return to work, school, or family immediately after each session.

Many patients notice improvements within the first few weeks, with lasting relief after the full course of treatment.
